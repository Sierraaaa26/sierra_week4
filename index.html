Sierra's Topics-Grapes


Section

On todays topic for the day, I will be talking about why I think grapes are the best fruit.

Stating My Case

Grapes are the best fruit because grapes quietly do everything well, for example they are easy to eat, no peeling, slicing, or cleanup. They are also portable so you can fit them anywhere. The texture is great, espically when its frozen and lastly they have great versatility. You can make things like juice, raisins, jam, desserts, salads, cheese boards, wine, and etc.

Rebutal

Now I know grapes have high sugar density, which is not the best. A cup of grapes contains a lot of natural sugar and is very easy to eat quickly but compared with other fruits like apples or oranges, grapes are less filling per calorie because they have less fiber relative to volume. Another thing is they are easy to overeat, most people don’t stop at 10 grapes. A large bowl can turn into hundreds of calories. Nonetheless, I believe grapes are great.

Second best fruit- Strawberries

While I do think grapes are the best, a close second for me is strawberries.
